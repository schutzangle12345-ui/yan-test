Our Story

Luxury access, uncomplicated.

BookLux is an AI-powered chat concierge designed to eliminate the friction from high-end travel and lifestyle management.

The complexity of exclusive bookings.

For too long, booking a private jet, a luxury villa, or a chartered yacht has meant navigating a maze of brokers, hidden fees, and endless paperwork. Busy clients and family offices shouldn't have to manage dozens of vendors just to plan a single itinerary.

We saw the transparency gap in the market. We saw the hours wasted on phone calls for simple price comparisons. We decided to fix it.

"Our mission is to return the most valuable asset to our clients: Time."
